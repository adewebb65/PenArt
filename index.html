About the Artist

Penny Webb

I’m Penny, an artist working in East Devon and producing abstract and figurative works using resin incorporating other media that fit with my ethos of creating pieces that ‘flow’.

What do I mean by flow? Simply that although I control the vision, I don’t control all the detail of the realisation. The use of resin often creates unpredictable and beautiful results. In this way, there is a strong parallel to the events of nature and biology.

As an artist, I am part of this process but there are also things beyond me going on in my art. It is a collaboration. I love this!

Over the years, as I’ve developed my styles I have undertaken regular commissions — both personal and commercial — including several very large pieces for the illustrious Howard de Walden Estate in London for a major Marylebone development. I exhibit regularly and aim to show a style that is still relatively under-represented to a wider audience. Every piece is a true ‘one off’ that brings something unique and eye-catching to the walls of my clients.

Commissions

Let’s Create Something Together

I am prepared to undertake commissioned work from small (40cm × 40cm) to VERY large. My largest to date was a huge agate design measuring two metres × 1.5 metres and weighing over 30kg!

If nothing catches your eye today, on Instagram I occasionally show new works in progress (because I’m usually too busy creating to do the social media curating!).

Thanks and I hope you enjoy my work.
